About Us

  INTRO of TAIWAN DEDRE-TECH

As a technology solutions leader, DEDRE-TECH values our partners. As a partner, We are successfully on all kinds of processing high standard on production, designing, producing, packaging, and shipment in variety metal products for different types of hardware and customized profession field products for years. Built on a foundation of operational excellence and innovative technology, DEDRE-TECH exceeds our commitment to our customers with responsive, reliable, and quick customer service for all our products.

 INTERGRATION & OEM/ODM

By combining engineering competence with design capabilities, DEDRE-TECH is a trustworthy partner for our OEM/ODM clients. We manufacture innovative products which deliver higher ROI with lower resource requirement, while maintaining the highest qualities. Our streamlined ODM workflow also accelerates time-to-market for our clients. From key components to complete mechanism systems, DEDRE-TECH provides unique design with our specialized technology, and achieving the certifications required by our customers is our goal. DEDRE-TECH also respects our customers' domain expertise and applies their concepts in tailor-made solutions. Our commitment in the OEM/ODM business will be your trustworthy and long-term partnerships.

 WE CARE SUSTAINABILITIES

We believe that the manufacturing of hardware should be done in a sustainable and responsible way. We constantly aim for less pollutive and more eco- friendly ways to develop our products. We choose our suppliers and methods with great care and high ethical standards. We care for the environment and finding the best solution for sustainability is the core of DEDRE-TECH.
